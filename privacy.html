Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Welcome to GetPhoneNum.com. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Our Phone Number Generator is a free web-based tool. We do not require user registration, and we do not collect, store, or track any personal identification information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or physical addresses from our visitors.

2. Log Files and Analytics

Like most websites, we use Google Analytics (GA4) to help us understand how visitors interact with our site. This service may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and the pages you visit. This data is used solely for improving our website performance and user experience.

3. Cookies and Advertising

We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. These third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Data Security

All phone numbers generated on this site are created using client-side or server-side random algorithms for testing and educational purposes only. These numbers are not real and are not linked to any actual telecommunications service.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us through our website domain's administrative email.
